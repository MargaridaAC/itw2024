Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is central to Paris 2024, hosting events like beach volleyball and framing the Seine River opening ceremony, symbolizing unity and Parisian heritage.

The Phryge

The Phryge, inspired by the Phrygian cap, is a historic French symbol of liberty and revolution, dating back to the French Revolution. Chosen as the Paris 2024 mascot, it embodies freedom, resilience, and the Olympic spirit, connecting France's rich history with the global celebration of sports.

Spectators

Paris 2024 will welcome global spectators with affordable tickets and public events, ensuring everyone can experience the Olympic spirit in iconic Parisian venues.

Triumph at the Finish Line

Ethiopian marathon runner Tola crosses the finish line with determination and pride, symbolizing resilience and excellence at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. A moment of victory celebrated on the road to Paris 2024.
