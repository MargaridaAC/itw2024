Countdown until July 14, 2028

The 2028 Summer Olympics will take place in Los Angeles, marking the third time the city hosts the event. Known for its iconic landmarks and diverse culture, LA promises to deliver a modern and sustainable Olympic Games. The city will feature both historic venues like the LA Memorial Coliseum and new facilities designed to leave a lasting legacy for the community. The LA 2028 Games will showcase a full range of Olympic sports, including athletics, swimming, and gymnastics, alongside emerging sports like surfing and skateboarding. With a focus on inclusivity and innovation, the Games aim to engage global audiences and inspire athletes to push the boundaries of excellence while promoting the Olympic values of friendship and respect.
